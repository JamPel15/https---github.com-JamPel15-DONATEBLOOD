Donate Blood in Buenavista

Blood donation in Buenavista plays a vital role in supporting the healthcare needs of this coastal municipality. Your donation helps maintain the blood supply for emergency cases, childbirth complications, and patients undergoing surgeries at local medical facilities.

Community Impact

Buenavista's blood donation program serves not just the town proper but also neighboring barangays. By donating here, you directly contribute to:

  • Local Healthcare: Supporting Buenavista District Hospital and rural health units
  • Fisherfolk Communities: Providing emergency blood for fishing-related accidents
  • Maternal Care: Helping mothers with pregnancy complications
