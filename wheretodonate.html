Where to donate blood


Giving blood

You can give blood at thousands of donation venues across Agusan del Norte, from permanent donor centres to pop-up community venues.

More about giving blood:
  • A blood donation is taken from your arm.
  • The appointment takes around an hour in total, including 10 to 15 minutes to donate, plus time to have a drink and a snack.

About our donation venues

Our city donor centres need you

We run most of our appointments at 27 main donor centres where we have permanent teams and greatest capacity. Right now, there are spaces we must fill in order to maintain a good blood supply to hospitals.

Our Agusan del Norte donor centres, especially Nasipit, Buenavista and Butuan, have the most available appointments.

Please book at one of these venues, if you can:


Nasipit:
  • Nasipit District Hospital, Agusan del Norte
  • Nasipit Municipal Health Office
  • Nasipit Community Center
Butuan:
  • Butuan Doctors' Hospital, Agusan del Norte
  • Butuan City Health Office
  • St. Therese Hospital, Butuan
Buenavista:
  • Buenavista Rural Health Unit, Agusan del Norte
  • Buenavista Municipal Health Center
  • Buenavista General Hospital
