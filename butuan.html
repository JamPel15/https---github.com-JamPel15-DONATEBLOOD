Donate Blood in Butuan City

Blood donation is a crucial humanitarian act that saves lives daily. In Butuan City, the demand for blood remains high to support medical procedures, emergency cases, and patients with chronic conditions requiring regular transfusions.

Why Donate in Butuan?

Butuan City serves as the regional center of Caraga, making its blood banks vital for the entire region. By donating here, you contribute to:

  • Regional Healthcare: Supporting not just Butuan but neighboring provinces as well
  • Emergency Preparedness: Ensuring blood is available for accidents and disasters
  • Medical Advancements: Helping local hospitals perform complex surgeries
