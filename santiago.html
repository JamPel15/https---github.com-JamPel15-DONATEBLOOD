Donate Blood in Santiago

Blood donation is a vital community service that helps save lives in Santiago. As an important municipality in Agusan del Norte, Santiago has a growing need for blood donations to support local healthcare services, emergency cases, and patients with chronic illnesses.

Health Benefits for Donors

Donating blood not only helps others but also provides benefits for donors:

  • Health Check: Each donation includes a free mini-physical that checks your blood pressure, hemoglobin levels, and more.
  • Reduced Iron Levels: Regular blood donation helps reduce iron stores in the body, which may lower heart disease risk.
  • Community Impact: Your donation directly helps neighbors and community members in need.
